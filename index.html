About

The best thing about “love” is that it has no age. It binds the world together with emotions. Isn’t being in love the best feeling ever? It brings out a poet from one's soul. Pursuit Of Love is the artworks of poets around the globe. It describes the writer’s perceptions to love. The ever-expanding spectrum of love cannot be described through a single definition, it needs boundless space and an infinite combination of words. This anthology consists of pristine love poetries and quotes that infuse emotional war into readers mind.
